Cruise

Looking for boat?

There is a boat for everyone, let's find yours. No matter your budget and numbers of friends, we’ve got you. Rent an luxury boat for just you and your squad or join others on an affordable boat cruise. Book a boat cruise in lekki, ikoyi or VI and enjoy Lagos beautiful sunsets any time, any day and for any number of persons.

Our packages are of 2 categories

Boat Transfer- Book a Boat to Ilashe beach, Tarkwa bay, Badagry or Eleko. Our transfer boats are safe and can take as many persons for your party at the beach.

Luxury Boat Cruise- Go on a luxury boat cruise in Lagos in any of our boats or luxurious Yatch and enjoy service at it’s best. With our luxurious boat cruise, you can cruise Lagos in 2 – 3 hours with drinks and finger food.
